About Us

History of CycleTrack

CycleTrack is a bicycle renting web application. When I was in the university, we used to rent bicycles and give our student IDs to the owner as a collateral and the owner used to claculate prices manually. CycleTrack aims to digitize this business by strreamlining the process of renting and rturning a bicycle and calculating the amount to be paid.
